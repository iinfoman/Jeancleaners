Your Safety Matters

Solo Cleaner. Safe Systems.

Jean works alone — which means safety protocols protect both of you. Here's how.

🪪

ID Verified & Badged

Jean carries a verified ID badge at all times. You can request to see her ID before any clean begins. No ID shown = no entry.

📍

Live Check-In System

Jean checks in via WhatsApp to a trusted contact on every job arrival and checks out on departure. If no check-out is received, a welfare check is triggered.

📸

Before & After Photos

Jean photographs the space on arrival and completion. This protects you from false damage claims and protects Jean from false accusations.

📋

Booking Records

Every booking is logged with your name, address and contact number. Records are kept securely. No anonymous jobs accepted.

🚫

Right to Decline

Jean reserves the right to decline or leave any job where she feels unsafe. Clients who are aggressive or inappropriate are blacklisted immediately.

📞

Emergency Contact

A trusted emergency contact has Jean's schedule at all times. In any incident, they can provide job location and client details to authorities.

💛

A note from Jean: "I take your safety and my safety seriously. These systems aren't just policies — they're how I do every single job. You deserve to trust who is in your home, and I deserve to feel safe doing my work."
